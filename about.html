About Us

The story behind every unforgettable event

Our Story

MAREA was born from a simple but powerful belief — that every moment worth celebrating deserves to be extraordinary. Founded over 12 years ago in Tirana, we have grown from a small passionate team into one of Albania's most trusted event planning studios.

We approach every event — whether an intimate birthday or a grand corporate gala — with the same obsessive attention to detail, creative thinking, and genuine care for our clients' visions.

Over the years, MAREA has planned more than 350 events across Albania and the Balkans, working with private clients, multinational corporations, and government institutions alike. Every project adds to our portfolio of cherished moments.
