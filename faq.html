PAYMENTS & CONTRACTS

We require a 30% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance split into milestone payments. The final payment is due 14 days before the event. We accept bank transfer and major credit/debit cards.
Cancellations made more than 90 days before the event receive a full refund minus the deposit. Cancellations within 90–30 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 30 days of the event are non-refundable. We strongly recommend event insurance for large bookings.
